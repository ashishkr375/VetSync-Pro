What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a personalized experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Stay on your device until they expire or you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by VetSync Pro directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by our service providers for analytics and marketing

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when you close the browser
